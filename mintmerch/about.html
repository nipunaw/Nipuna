ABOUT US

Mint Merch is a non-profit organization whose mission is to support a local FBLA chapter in their endeavor to attend the National Leadership Conference. Based out of Tampa, Florida, we provide quality, donated goods to consumers that want to facilitate the academic success of local students. Every year, Mint Merch provides local chapters with funding and sends more well-learned students to The FBLA National Leadership Conference, giving them the chance to hold their own against a diverse pool of fellow students. Mint Merch gives these students opportunities that they otherwise could not have afforded.

Whether you're looking for a bag to take to school or some chic accessories for your morning and nightly commute, Mint Merch has a product for you. Our company makes quality and modern style a top priority. Since our products are donated and rebranded locally, making a purchase directly contributes to the local economy and benefits students and education. We ensure that our products are selected with care and are of the finest materials. So don't be fooled by our cool candy hues, the condition of our products is miles above “mint”!

SHIPPING POLICY

• All products purchased are shipped and handled standardly. Please allow 6-8 business days from the date the package was shipped. Your purchase will take no more than 48 hours to process before sending out to ship.

• We accept return requests via the "Contact Us" form found here. Return requests can be submitted within 7 days of receiving the product(s). Shipping costs will not be covered except in cases of damage. Please note that you must specify whether you want a replacement or a refund.

• Any product shipped from us is guaranteed to have undergone company rebranding and a verification process to ensure integrity. A certification form should be included with every shipment that is delivered. If such is not the case, send us an inquiry.
